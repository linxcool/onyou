Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all games published by ONYOU LTD. ("we", "us", or "our") on the Meta Instant Games platform, including but not limited to Onyou and any other titles we release under the Onyou brand (collectively, "our Games").

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use our Games, how we process and share it, and how you can exercise your rights over that data. It applies to all users who can access our Games through the Meta Instant Games platform, regardless of your location, login method, or age.

When contacting us about a specific game, please mention the game name so we can assist you promptly.

1. Serverless Architecture — How We Handle Data

Our Games operate on a serverless model. We do not maintain our own backend servers or databases to store player data. Instead:

  • Game progress and player data are stored by the Meta Instant Games platform itself, using Meta's built-in SDK APIs (e.g., player data storage, leaderboards, contextual data). We do not independently collect or warehouse this information on external servers.
  • What this means for you: Most personal data associated with your gameplay is held and managed by Meta, not by us. When you remove a game from your Facebook account or delete your Facebook account, the platform-level data is handled according to Meta's Data Policy (https://www.facebook.com/policy.php).
  • What we may process temporarily: In the normal course of gameplay, your device may send requests that pass through game logic running in your browser or on Meta's infrastructure. Any data processed this way is ephemeral — used only to deliver the current game session — and is not persisted by us.

This architecture means that our data collection and storage footprint is inherently minimal. For the avoidance of doubt, this Privacy Policy covers any data we do process, but in practice the majority of your game data resides with Meta.

6. Data Retention

As described in Section 1, our Games follow a serverless model. We do not operate backend databases that persist player data independently. Data accessed during gameplay is:

  • Player data stored by Meta: Retained according to Meta's Data Policy. Players can manage or delete this data via their Facebook account settings at https://www.facebook.com/settings.
  • Ephemeral session data: Any data processed in the browser or on Meta's infrastructure during a game session is transient — it exists only for the duration of that session and is not stored by us.
  • Email correspondence: If you contact us (e.g., for a data deletion request), your email and any information you provide will be retained only as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry, up to a maximum of 90 days after resolution.

7. Data Deletion Rights — All User Groups

7.1 Overview

Regardless of your country/region, login method, or age, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data that we hold. This covers data collected across all our Games. If you wish to delete data for a specific game only, please mention the game name in your request. Specific steps for each user group are detailed below.

7.2 By Region / Jurisdiction

EU / EEA Users (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Erasure (Article 17): Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest

How to request deletion:

  1. Email: Send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with subject "GDPR Data Deletion Request"
  2. In-game: Go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete My Data"
  3. Response time: We will respond within 30 days
  4. Right to complain: If unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority

California, USA Users (CCPA/CPRA)

Under CCPA/CPRA, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected from you
  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories of personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (for cross-context behavioral advertising)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You will not face discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

How to request deletion:

  1. Email: Send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with subject "CCPA Data Deletion Request"
  2. In-game: Go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete My Data"
  3. Response time: We will respond within 45 days (extendable to 90 days, with notice)
  4. Authorized agent: You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf; we may require verification of the agent's authorization

Brazil Users (LGPD)

Under LGPD, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of unnecessary or improperly processed personal data
  • Right of Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data
  • Right to Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Right to Explanation: Request an explanation of automated decision-making

How to request deletion:

  1. Email: Send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with subject "LGPD Data Deletion Request"
  2. In-game: Go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete My Data"
  3. Response time: We will confirm receipt and begin processing within 15 days

All Other Regions

If you are located outside the regions listed above (including but not limited to the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Japan, South Korea, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and Latin America), you also have the right to request deletion of your personal data:

How to request deletion:

  1. Email: Send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request", including your country/region and username in the email body
  2. In-game: Go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete My Data"
  3. Response time: We will respond within 30 days
Regardless of where you are, simply send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com saying "I want to delete my data" and we will process it. We do not treat you differently based on your region.

7.3 By Login Method

Users Logged in via Facebook

  1. In the game, go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete My Data"
  2. Or send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with your Facebook username or associated email
  3. You may also revoke any of our Games' data access via Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove the Game (note: this does not automatically delete data we have already collected; please submit a deletion request through one of the methods above as well)

Users Accessing via Messenger

  1. Send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with your Messenger username
  2. Or use the in-game menu within Messenger → Settings → "Delete My Data"

Guest / Non-Logged-In Users

Even if you play in guest mode (without linking a Facebook account), you have the right to data deletion:

  1. Send an email to onyoufun@gmail.com with your device model, approximate play time period, the game name, and in-game nickname (if applicable)
  2. We will use the information you provide to locate and delete the associated data
  3. Note: Guest data is stored locally on your device; uninstalling the game or clearing your browser cache will also remove most of this data

7.4 By Age Bracket

Children Under 13 (COPPA Protection)

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please email onyoufun@gmail.com
  • Use subject: "COPPA — Child Data Deletion Request"
  • We will verify and delete the relevant data within 48 hours

Teens Aged 13–16 (GDPR Member States)

  • Where consent is required for data processing, we require users to meet the digital age of consent (typically 16, or 13 in certain member states)
  • If you or your guardian wish to request data deletion, follow the steps for your region in Section 7.2
  • Guardians using subject "Parental — Data Deletion Request" will receive priority processing

Adult Users (16 and above)

Follow the steps for your region (Section 7.2) or login method (Section 7.3) directly.

9. Post-Deletion Processing

After we receive and verify your deletion request:

  1. Immediate cessation: Stop collecting and processing your data
  2. Core data deletion: Delete personal data within the applicable response window (15–45 days, by region)
  3. Backup deletion: Data in backup systems is purged in the next backup cycle (maximum 90 days)
  4. Retention exceptions: Data may be retained only where:
    • Required by legal obligation (e.g., payment records under tax law)
    • Necessary for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims
    • Necessary to prevent fraud or abuse

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including Transport Layer Security (TLS) encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews.
